steven is a digital web producer

Steven is a lead agile producer and works with big brands in retail and financial services. Collaborating with various teams, he delivers ecommerce websites utilising Magento, Drupal and Salesforce Marketing Cloud platforms.

Steven works with a solutions led team and have a great eye for details and responsibility for rolling out ecommerce solutions. Steven's involvement includes everything from strategy, scope, creation to delivery. Steven's strong point are his time management and organisation skills.

Steven is a certified scrum master and professional project manager. With a background in agile software development, networking and systems architecture, Steven enjoys building mashup web applications and release stable functional systems quickly.

Steven's usual appetites are in improving delivery of business intelligence, enhancing process efficiency and making customers happy. At leisure, Steven catches up on news in Bloomberg BusinessWeek, Business Insider and Mashable among others to stay abreast with business and technology trends.
